English

Bloom collects no personal data. Bloom is a minimal water‑reminder app. It has no account, no login, no tracking, no advertising, and no third‑party analytics or SDKs.

What Bloom stores

  • Bloom records only how many times you confirmed a reminder each day — not amounts, and no health data.
  • This data is stored on your device.
  • If you are signed in to iCloud, the data may sync through your own private iCloud (CloudKit) database, managed by Apple under your Apple ID. The developer has no access to it. This is iCloud sync, not data collection.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No reminder content is sent to the developer or any third party.

Data sharing

None. No data is transmitted to the developer or to any third party. There are no ads and no analytics.

Children

Bloom is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions? Email eyou.apps@yahoo.com.
